Our Coffee Menu

Cappuccino

A creamy cappuccino made with espresso and rich milk foam, ideal for coffee lovers with a balanced taste and smooth texture.

Ice Coffee

A refreshing iced coffee that combines cold espresso with milk or cold water, offering a cool and refreshing taste perfect for hot summer days.

Turkish Coffee

Turkish coffee is rich in flavor and prepared in the traditional way by grinding the coffee beans very finely and boiling them slowly with water and sugar to taste. It is served with a thick foam and a strong, distinctive taste.

Affogato Coffee

Vanilla ice cream is generally topped with a clear espresso shot – dessert and coffee in one.

Macchiato Coffee

Espresso with a small drop of milk added, strong in flavor and preferred by lovers of strong coffee.

Flat White Coffee

A mixture of espresso and steamed milk, like a latte but with a lighter, smoother layer of foam.
